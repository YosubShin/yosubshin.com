Tablet Magazine

This is a mock magazine designed for tablet devices. I borrowed ideas from Apple Watch for the main navigation, and also used a circular interface to display a preview of an article. On the main navigation page, there is a hidden menu bar on the left side which is supposed to pop out once a user swipes right from the left edge of the screen.

Now that I look back what I made almost a year ago, I see so much room for improvement. For example, by justifying the text in the article view, the text is totally distracting. Small caps for the opening paragraph is equally ugly and should be undone.
